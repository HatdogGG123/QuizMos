To begin the game, you must pick first your character and then click the start button to start the game. You will have a random monster opponent that you need to defeat to win the game. If your answer is wrong your enemy will attack you and your HP bar will decrease but if your answer is correct, your character will attack the enemy. The questions are arranged randomly. The game will end if you or your enemy is defeated.
