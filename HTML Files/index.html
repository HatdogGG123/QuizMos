Quizmos is a website provided for students who want to study lessons related to programming. It contains modules or learning materials that can be downloaded for offline access. Additionally, it offers different types of interactive quizzes such as Quiz io, Hangman, and Codemaster, where quiz takers can receive badges to help them practice their learning and knowledge from the provided materials.

How to use QuizMos?

1. Click the GET STARTED button.

2. Create an account by clicking “SIGN UP HERE” link below, then input your USERNAME and PASSWORD, ensuring they match and are memorable.

3. Once done, it will return to its initial state before you SIGN IN. Use the same USERNAME and PASSWORD you entered during SIGN IN, then click the LOG IN button.

4. Now that you're logged into your account, you can access modules and games.

5. In the modules, click the FILE LINK to choose whether to download a pdf or ppt for having an offline saved files.

6. For the games, start by reading the game mechanics to understand how to play. After playing, input your username and click the submit button or hit enter on you keyboard for us to track your scores. Don't worry, the more you try, the more you succeed. So feel free to repeat answering as many times as you need.You can also check your rank on the leaderboards feature of our website

7. When you're done using QuizMos, you can log out by clicking the LOGOUT BUTTON for those who use desktops, laptops or tablets at the bottom left of you screen and for those who use their cellphones you can click the LOGOUT ICON at the top right of your screen.
